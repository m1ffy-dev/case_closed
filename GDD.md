# Case Closed?

## Elevator Pitch

A cozy mystery game where a rookie private investigator solves small-town cases
while uncovering the biggest secret in the towns history.

## Genre

Cozy Mystery
Narrative Adventure
Puzzle

## Setting

Maple Creek

Population: ~3,000

A quiet rural town where everybody knows everybody.

## Gameplay Loop

1. Receive a case
2. Gather clues
3. solve puzzles to get evidence
4. Interview suspects
5. Solve the mystery

## Art Style

- Cute
- Cozy
- Storybook-inspired
- Warm colours
