## main character/player -  Bambi Bennet
Occupation: Private Investigator

Age: 22

Description:
bambi grew up in appleby creek, a town famous for its apple farms, and has always had 
a penchant for solving mysterys, like her dad, the local police chief, 
may it be her mothers missing watch(the family dog buried it) 
or the missing funds from the school fundraiser, and that led her to joining the police academy at 18.
However after only a year on the force, her father broke his knee/
so Bambi had to come home and help her dad, which led her to opening her own private investigation business. 
Of course, everyone in the town still sees her as little ol' bambi following her father at the station, 
but shes determined to prove everyone wrong



## Honey Halloway
Occupation: Baker

Age: 25

Description:
Honey Halloway runs the local bakery, where she sells all kinds of apple baked goods.
She's engaged to Richard Rodgers, a local officer.
She started baking after her mom died in a car accident and is determined to make a warm space, 
free day-old goods to those in need, and a friendly ear 

## Richard Rodgers
Occupation: Police Officer

Age: 27

Description:
A local police officer engaged to Honey Halloway. 
He's a "returnee", was the towns prodigal football player and left for the city to join the police, and 
became a SWAT officer, however after an injury he returned for a quieter, safer life. 

## Avery Adams
Occupation: Doctor

Age: 35

Description:
The towns doctor, her husbands a pilot and often stays away from town for weeks at a time
because of that she's raised her son alexander as a single mom.
She wasn't originally from appleby creek, once she finished her residency she realised small towns 
don't get the best medical care, and she decided to come to her husbands home town
