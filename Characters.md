## main character/player -  Bambi Sen
Occupation: Private Investigator

Age: 22

Description:
bambi grew up in appleby creek, a town famous for its apple farms, and has always had 
a penchant for solving mysterys, or the missing funds from the school fundraiser, 
and that led her to joining the police academy at 18.
However after only a year on the force, her father broke his knee/
so Bambi had to come home and help her dad, which led her to opening her own private investigation business. 
Of course, everyone in the town still sees her as little ol' bambi following her father at the station, 
but shes determined to prove everyone wrong
